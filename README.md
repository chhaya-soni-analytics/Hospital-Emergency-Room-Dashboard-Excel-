# 🏥 Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive **Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard** designed to analyze patient flow, waiting time, satisfaction levels, and departmental referrals.

The dashboard helps healthcare professionals and administrators monitor emergency room performance and identify areas for improvement.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze patient volume and trends
* Monitor average waiting time
* Evaluate patient satisfaction levels
* Track admission vs non-admission rates
* Analyze patient distribution across age groups and departments

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* 👥 **Total Patients:** 9,216
* ⏱ **Average Wait Time:** 35.26 minutes
* ⭐ **Patient Satisfaction Score:** 4.99 / 5

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 📅 Monthly Patient Trends

* Track patient inflow across months
* Identify peak and low-demand periods

---

### 🚑 Admission Status Analysis

* 50% patients admitted
* 50% patients not admitted
* Balanced distribution helps evaluate ER load

---

### ⏳ Patient Attend Status

* Comparison of **On-Time vs Delayed cases**
* Helps monitor operational efficiency

---

### 👨‍⚕️ Department Referral Analysis

* Highest referrals in **General Practice**
* Followed by Orthopedics, Cardiology, and other departments
* Highlights workload distribution across departments

---

### 👥 Gender Analysis

* Nearly equal distribution between male and female patients
* Indicates unbiased patient intake

---

### 🎂 Age Group Analysis

* Highest patient volume in middle age groups (20–40 years)
* Useful for targeted healthcare planning

---

4. Analyze trends and performance

## 📌 Key Insights

* Patient inflow is consistent with slight monthly variations
* Average waiting time indicates scope for operational improvement
* Equal admission rate suggests balanced triage decisions
* Certain departments face higher load and require resource optimization
* Middle-aged population contributes most to ER visits

---

## 📌 Conclusion

This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of emergency room operations. It highlights key performance indicators such as patient load, waiting time, and satisfaction levels.

The insights suggest that improving operational efficiency, reducing delays, and optimizing department workloads can significantly enhance patient experience and healthcare outcomes.
